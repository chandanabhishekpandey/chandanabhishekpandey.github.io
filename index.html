a Recent Ph.D. Graduate

I have completed my Ph.D. in Spintronics at Fert Beijing Research Institute under the supervision of Professor Weisheng Zhao's Group

EXPERIENCE

Postdoctoral Researcher

Experimental physicist at CIC nanoGUNE, San Sebastián, working on magneto-thermo-plasmonics, light–matter interaction, and artificial spin-ice systems. Focus on nanoscale heat-assisted magnetic phenomena and their application to emergent quantum and topological states.

Nanofabrication & Laboratory In-Charge

Responsible for operation, maintenance, and user training of nanofabrication facilities. Hands-on expertise in e-beam lithography, AJA sputtering systems, thin-film deposition, and process optimization for spintronic and nanomagnetic devices.

Spintronics & Magnetic Nanodevices Researcher

Extensive experience in CoFeB/MgO-based MTJs, antiferromagnetic materials, and nanoscale magnetic characterization using SQUID-VSM, nanoMOKE, and magneto-optical techniques. Strong background in device physics and experimental design.

Doctoral Researcher (PhD)

PhD in Microelectronics and Solid-State Electronics, focused on spintronic devices and magnetic tunnel junctions. Research combined device fabrication, magnetic characterization, and theoretical understanding of nanoscale magnetic phenomena.
